Aubrey Murdock is Head of School for the University of Orange, a free people's school that operates on the principle that everyone has something to teach and everyone has something to learn. Murdock is a graduate of the Design & Urban Ecologies program at Parsons The New School (M.S.) and Columbia College (B.A., Film Production). She focuses on the role of media and design within civic education and involvement.
